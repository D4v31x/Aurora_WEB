Privacy Policy

Introduction

Aurora Music ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Aurora Music (the "App").

By using Aurora Music, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Aurora Music stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User Preferences: Theme settings, language preferences, display options, and app configuration
  • Listening History: Your recently played tracks (up to 1,000 events) to provide personalized suggestions
  • Play Statistics: Play counts for tracks, albums, artists, and playlists to improve recommendations
  • Playlists: User-created playlists and liked songs
  • Cached Data: Album artwork, artist images, and lyrics cached for offline access and performance
  • App Settings: Audio playback preferences, notification settings, and UI customizations

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Analytics Data (Microsoft Clarity)

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how users interact with our app. Clarity collects:

  • Anonymous usage patterns and session recordings
  • UI interaction heatmaps
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash and error reports

Clarity does not collect personally identifiable information. All data is anonymized and aggregated. You can learn more about Microsoft Clarity's privacy practices at clarity.microsoft.com/terms.

3. Error Reports

When the app encounters errors, we collect:

  • Error messages and stack traces
  • Timestamp of the error
  • Device and app version information

Error data is stored locally and may be sent anonymously to help us improve app stability.

Permissions We Request

Aurora Music requests the following permissions to function properly:

Storage / Media Audio Required to access and play music files stored on your device
Internet Required to fetch album artwork, lyrics, and music metadata from online services
Notifications Optional - to display playback controls in your notification bar
Bluetooth Optional - to support Bluetooth audio devices and controls
Foreground Service Required to continue audio playback when the app is in the background

Third-Party Services

Aurora Music connects to the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

Lyrics Service

  • Provider: LRCLib (lrclib.net)
  • Data Sent: Song title and artist name
  • Purpose: Fetching synchronized lyrics for your music
  • Privacy Policy: lrclib.net

Album Artwork & Metadata

  • Providers: Deezer API, iTunes/Apple Music API
  • Data Sent: Search queries containing song/album/artist names
  • Purpose: Fetching album artwork and music metadata
  • Privacy Policies: Deezer, Apple

Artist Images (Optional)

  • Provider: Spotify API (only if configured with API keys)
  • Data Sent: Artist names
  • Purpose: Fetching artist profile images
  • Privacy Policy: spotify.com/privacy

Donation Platforms (Optional)

If you choose to support development through donations, you may be redirected to:

  • Ko-fi (ko-fi.com/privacy)
  • Buy Me a Coffee (buymeacoffee.com/privacy-policy)

These platforms have their own privacy policies, and we do not receive any personal payment information.

Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Local Storage: All personal data (playlists, preferences, listening history) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers
  • No Account Required: Aurora Music does not require user registration or login
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device and is not synced to cloud services
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive data uses the platform's secure storage mechanisms

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you uninstall the app or manually clear app data
  • Listening History: Limited to the most recent 1,000 events
  • Cached Content: Artwork and lyrics cache can be cleared through app settings
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Microsoft Clarity's data retention policies

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Clear Your Data

  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Clear app cache through your device settings
  • Clear specific data (playlists, history) through app settings

Opt-Out Options

  • Disable notification permission to prevent notification-based controls
  • Disable internet access to prevent any external data fetching (lyrics, artwork will be unavailable)
  • Use the app offline to minimize data transmission

Control Analytics

While analytics are anonymized, you can limit data collection by:

  • Using the app in offline mode where possible
  • Reviewing your device's privacy settings for app permissions

Children's Privacy

Aurora Music is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is a music player for locally stored content and does not contain age-restricted features.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notice within the app for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Open Source

Aurora Music is developed as an open-source project. You can review the source code to verify our privacy practices at our GitHub repository.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Developer: Aurora Software CZ
