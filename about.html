Story

The Beginning

Aurora Software started as a passion project by a music enthusiast who wanted a better way to experience music on Android. Frustrated with existing players lacking personality and modern design, the journey to create Aurora Music began.

What started as a personal project has grown into a commitment to deliver the most beautiful and functional music player available.

The Creator

David

Developer & Designer

A passionate student developer with a love for music and beautiful interfaces. Combining technical skills with creative vision to build Aurora Music from the ground up.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Is Aurora Music free?

Yes! Aurora Music is completely free and open source. No ads, no subscriptions, no hidden costs.

What platforms are supported?

Currently Aurora Music is available for Android. iOS and desktop versions are planned for future development.

Where does the music data come from?

Aurora Music plays your local music files. Lyrics and metadata are fetched from various online sources when available.
